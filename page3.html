I don't want to downgrade.

When you go tiny it can seem like you might have to give a lot of things up, but really when you focus on the things you care about going tiny can be an upgrade to the lifestyle you really want to live.

Do you love cooking? Then let’s put a chef’s dream kitchen in your home. Do you love rockclimbing? Then we will transform the outside of your home into a rockclimbing wall.

We focus on the individual needs and dreams of our clients and bring them to life. We’ve showcased a few of our favorite projects to show you how we’ve upgraded our client’s lives.

A funky Craftsman.

Sue and Mark came to us looking for a craftman with some unique flair. They are both working artists and needed a space that reflected that as well as space to make their work. We implimented some great storage solutions to create a space that can transform into an art studio in just a few minutes.

Our biggest kitchen yet.

Kelly runs a cooking and health blog so she needed a kitchen unlike any we had created before. This impressive space is bigger than a lot we’ve seen in traditional homes, and works perfectly as a space for Kelly to try out new recipes or photograph her favorite ones.

Ready for adventure.

Liz and Nick have a passion for outdoor adventure whether it be hiking, rock climbing, or biking. We created this home so they can bring their sense of adventure everywhere they go.

Tiny live/work.

Angela wanted the freedom to start a business from home but was worried about how to fit both a live and work space in a tiny home. We worked closely with her to create a space where she could grow her business in style.
